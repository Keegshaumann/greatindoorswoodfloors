Hardwood floor installation in Indianapolis

New wood floors for existing homes and new construction. Most of our installs are remodeling projects: new hardwood, matched so closely to your existing floors that the transition all but disappears.

Call (317) 403-1526 See our work

New wood over almost any surface

There's usually no need to tear out what's already down. We install hardwood floors over virtually any surface, from bare concrete to old ceramic tile.

We're also expert at matching new hardwood to an existing wood floor, pairing the wood type, color, and grain so that little or no sign of transition is visible.

Surfaces we install over

  • Existing wood floors
  • Concrete slabs
  • Old sub-flooring
  • Existing linoleum tile
  • Existing ceramic tile

For builders and remodelers

We work as a sub-contractor to custom remodelers and home builders, with flexible scheduling that fits your project timeline.

On new construction we coordinate with construction managers so floors go in on schedule, but not so early that other trades damage the new wood.

Remodels often pair a new install with refinishing of the home's original floors, or engineered flooring where the subfloor calls for it.
