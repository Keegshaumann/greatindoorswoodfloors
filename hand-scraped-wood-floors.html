Hand-scraped floors, rustic by design

We scrape the surface of each plank with hand tools, so no two boards look alike. The result is a wood floor that feels rustic, homey, and full of character.

Call (317) 403-1526 See our work

What hand-scraping does

Hand-scraping creates the look of a naturally distressed wood floor. We work the surface with hand tools, giving every plank its own texture and personality.

Already have dents, scratches, or years of wear? Hand-scraping turns that old damage into part of the floor's charm and patina.

It's labor-intensive work, done plank by plank, and that's exactly why no factory finish looks quite like it.

  • One-of-a-kind planks. Each board gets its own look and texture.
  • Rustic and homey. Warmth and depth you can see and feel.
  • Damage becomes character. Old wear reads as charm, not flaws.
  • Two ways to get there. Scrape your existing floor or install pre-scraped planks.

Scrape what you have, or start fresh

We can hand-scrape your existing wood floors right in place, working the texture into boards you already own.

Prefer new flooring? We also install planks that arrive already scraped, with the texture and depth built in.

Either way, we'll stain and finish the floor to your taste. Browse stain colors to start picturing it.

More ways to add character

Hand-scraping is just one of the looks we offer. We also do wire-brushing, whitewashing, oil finishes, creative staining patterns, and hand-made inlays and mosaics.

Curious how hand-scraping would look in your home?

Call for a free consultation. We'll give you an honest take on scraping your existing floors versus installing pre-scraped planks.
