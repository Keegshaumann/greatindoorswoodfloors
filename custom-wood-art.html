Beyond wood floors: custom wood art

Brian Depp's passion for beautiful woods is what led him into the floor business. It never stopped there. His home workshop turns out one-of-a-kind pieces, and he'll build anything you can imagine in wood.

Call (317) 403-1526 Email Brian your idea

The story of the red beech table

When Brian bought his house, a giant red beech stood where the garage had to go. A 25-foot stump, really, with a couple of small branches still putting out leaves.

He loves big old trees, so instead of hauling it away he had a portable sawmill slice the trunk. Beautiful random patterns appeared with every cut. The tree had been struggling for a long time, and the wood shows the beauty of it.

The result is this large end-grain table: red beech wrapped over a 1.5-inch plywood core, sturdy enough to support a couple of people yet not as heavy as it looks.

It was built in the shop that now stands where the tree stood for probably 150 years.
