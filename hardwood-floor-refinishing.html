Hardwood floor refinishing in Indianapolis

We've refinished thousands of floors across central Indiana since 1994. Sanded 99% dust-free, stained to your taste, and sealed with finishes tough enough for real life.

Call (317) 403-1526 See our work

Dust-free, fast, and built to last

Our 99% dust-free sanding system almost completely eliminates the wood dust that refinishing normally spreads through a house. Many clients even schedule the work while they are away on vacation, and those who stay can live at home during the job.

We generally use water-based finishes that dry quickly and are extremely durable. You can walk on the floor lightly within 1–2 hours of the final coat, and move furniture back within 12–24 hours.

If your floors are in good shape and just look tired, ask about maintenance coating, a lighter, less expensive refresh.

A two-day project, start to finish

A typical residential refinish takes two days. Larger homes, extra repairs, or custom color work can add a third.

Day One Repair, sand, stain

  • Repair any damaged wood before sanding
  • Sand the floor down to fresh, raw wood
  • Sample stain colors directly on your floor
  • Apply the stain and let it dry overnight

Day Two Inspect and finish

  • Inspect the dried stain for evenness
  • Apply the durable polyurethane topcoat
  • Light foot traffic in 1–2 hours, furniture in 12–24

Any color you can imagine

We carry swatches from Bona, Duraseal, and Minwax, and we mix custom colors when nothing off the shelf fits. We'll sample colors right on your freshly sanded wood so you see exactly how the stain reads in your light, on your floor.

Specialty looks are our specialty too: whitewashing, wire-brushing, hand-scraping, oil finishes, and inlays.
