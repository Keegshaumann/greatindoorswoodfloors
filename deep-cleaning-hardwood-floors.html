Deep cleaning that reaches into the grain

Years of foot traffic press dirt, dust, and grime down into the pores of a wood floor, leaving it dull or stained. Our power scrubbing process lifts it back out.

Call (317) 403-1526 See our work

How it works

We use a power scrubber with specially designed brushes and squeegees, paired with an environmentally friendly cleaning solution.

The brushes follow the grain of the wood, working the solution deep into the pores where ordinary mopping never reaches.

For damage cleaning can't fix, like gouges or water stains, see our repair service.

  • Reaches into the grain. Removes particles of dirt, dust, and grime lodged in the porous surface by years of traffic.
  • Safe for your finish. The process does not damage water-based polyurethane or oil-based finishes. Only the ground-in particles come out.
  • Stains improve too. Many stains can be completely removed or made much less noticeable.

Before and after

Floors we have deep cleaned. We matched the grain in each pair as closely as possible, so you're comparing the same boards honestly.

Pair it with a maintenance coat

On floors in generally good condition, deep cleaning is step one of our maintenance coating service.

After the cleaning, we lightly sand to roughen the existing polyurethane, then apply a fresh water-based clearcoat.

That combination can preserve your floors for years and spare you a full refinishing, which means sanding down to raw wood.

Dull floors might just be dirty floors

Call for a free look. We'll tell you honestly whether a deep clean, a maintenance coat, or a full refinish is the right fix.
