Wire-brushed floors with texture and character

Wire-brushing strips the softer wood from the surface of every plank, exposing the grain underneath. What's left is texture you can see and feel.

Call (317) 403-1526 See our work

What wire-brushing actually does

A stiff wire brush works across each plank, removing the softer wood from the surface. Only the hardest wood remains.

That exposes the wood's grain, creates texture, and highlights the natural beauty that was hiding in your boards all along.

It's a popular look for good reason: the floor feels weathered and earthy, like wood that has aged gracefully.

Why homeowners love wire-brushed floors

The texture does more than look good. It changes how the floor lives, day to day.

  • A weathered, earthy look. Wire-brushed wood has the rustic appearance of aged wood, lived-in from day one.
  • Knots and grain stand out. Brushing enhances the wood's natural features, adding texture and character to every plank.
  • Easier to live with. Little nicks and scratches blend in and become part of the charm. Great for kids and pets.
  • Better traction. The ridged surface gives your feet more grip than a slick, featureless finish.

Pair the texture with a finish you love

Wire-brushing plays well with other looks. Work whitewash into the open grain for a sun-bleached effect, or protect the texture with a natural oil finish. And if texture is what draws you in, check out hand-scraping too, another way we give floors character.
